EAGLE

Fun Facts

Eagles are powerful bird that owns the sky,they are also expert hunters, have excellent vision and their wingspan is seriously impressive. Their grip is ten times stronger than humans and they build their nest on top of high cliffs.They are a symbol of freedom and peace.They have a hooked beak which is great for hunting.A baby eagle is known as EAGLET. A group of eagle is called a covocation.They mate for life.
